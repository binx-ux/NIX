Aimbot

Sticky aim with angle decomposition, pitch clamping, and per-game bone targeting. Fully adjustable in the GUI.

ESP / Visuals

Player boxes, health bars, distance, names, and chams. Dynamic tab that rebuilds per-game on join.

▲

Movement

Infinite jump, noclip, auto sprint, click-to-teleport, and speed hacks. Toggled cleanly without breaking game state.

◇

Misc Hacks

Auto collect, time freeze, trigger bot. Game-specific scripts auto-loaded when supported place IDs are detected.

Lazy Tab Loading

Tabs only initialize when opened. Keeps the executor fast and the GUI responsive regardless of hub size.

External Script Loader

We officially support Xeno as the primary executor. It is keyless, actively maintained, and has the highest UNC compatibility with our feature set. Download it at xeno.now.

The hub runs on most Level 7 executors, but Xeno is the safest and most stable option. Other executors may cause partial feature breakage depending on their UNC support level.
